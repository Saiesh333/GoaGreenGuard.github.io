How to Organize a Beach Cleanup

It is a simple yet impactful way to protect our coastlines and engage the community. Start by choosing a local beach that needs attention and get the necessary permissions from authorities. Gather volunteers, provide them with gloves, bags, and safety instructions, and work together to remove litter and sort recyclable materials. Document the waste collected and share the results to raise awareness. These cleanups not only restore natural beauty but also inspire long-term environmental responsibility.

Reporting Guidelines

Ensure quick and effective action, provide clear and accurate details when reporting environmental issues. Mention the exact location, type of pollution (e.g., plastic waste, sewage, illegal dumping), and any visual evidence such as photos or videos. Be respectful and concise in your description. Avoid false or duplicate reports. Each submission is verified by our team or local partners, so truthful reporting helps us maintain credibility and respond faster to real problems..
